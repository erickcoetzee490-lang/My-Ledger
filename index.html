The one rule
Every Friday. 10 minutes. No exceptions.
Open this file every Friday evening. Enter your actuals for the week. That single habit is worth more than every feature in this tool. Rockefeller did this his entire life.
Your weekly habit — step by step
1.Open the Budget tab — update the Actual column for every expense you had this week. Be honest.
2.Check the Debt tab — see your recommended payment for the month. Pay it.
3.Log anything unplanned in the Impulse tab before you buy it. Wait 48 hours.
4.Log any side project earnings in the Commission tab under Extra Income.
Your monthly habit — end of month
1.Complete the Monthly Review — answer all 6 questions honestly and save it.
2.Update your debt balances in the Debt tab — type in what you actually owe now.
3.Update saved amounts in the Savings tab — what's actually in Simonis Storm now.
4.Hit Record this month in the Net Worth tab — locks your net worth for history.
5.Use the arrow → to move to next month. Your data is saved automatically.
